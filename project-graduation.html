Project 11

Design of an Intelligent Dashboard for Managing Group Adaptation in a CSCL Environment

Université 8 Mai 1945, Guelma

PHP Node.js MySQL Chart.js D3.js CSCL Learning Analytics Data Visualization

Problem Statement

The Problem

In CSCL (Computer-Supported Collaborative Learning) environments, group formation and management play a critical role in the success of collaborative activities. Yet teachers rarely have intuitive tools to monitor group dynamics in real time, analyze interactions, detect imbalances — disengagement, overload, isolation — or effectively adapt group composition. The absence of a visual and decision-support system limits teachers' ability to optimize collaboration between learners.

Our Goal

Develop an intelligent dashboard that facilitates the management, analysis, and adaptation of learner groups within a CSCL system — giving teachers real-time visibility and actionable recommendations to improve collaborative learning outcomes.

Participation

🤝
Cohesion
Evaluates the strength of inter-member relationships and group solidarity over time.
🎯
Performance
Monitors group-level and individual task completion, quality, and progress rates.

Synthesis of results. Limitations and perspectives — AI integration, real-time adaptation, full automation.
